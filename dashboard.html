Thanks to your contributions, CESSDA has now collected key performance indicators (C-KPIs) for 2025 to measure our progress and impact within the European Research Area.

This dashboard supports the validation of data. Here are some general instructions:

  • Left sidebar: Select your country to view the SP-specific dashboard
  • Top right corner […]: Download your dashboard as an image or view source code
  • Legends:
    • Blue: Validated data
    • Grey: 2025 data pending validation
    • Red: Missing/unavailable data (displayed as outlier)

Note: Please refer to the validation Excel file for the categorical KPIs (i.e., C-KPI-07, 09, 18, 19a)

If you identified any errors, please add comments directly in the Excel file.

Moreover, please complete this short 3-question feedback survey by [date]. One response per SP is sufficient.

If you have any questions, please contact Lorraine Wong, Senior Science Officer at the CESSDA Main Office lorraine.wong@cessda.eu.

Thank you for your time and input!
